The Job Workshop — Progress Tracker

Quick setup guide for Success Coaches

Your link: https://tjw-progress-tracker.onrender.com
  1. Log in — open the link and click your name.
  2. Add your clients — click + Add Client for each active client. Enter their name, start date, and which modules (M1/M2/M3) are already done.
  3. Backfill past weeks — click All Weeks on any client card and log numbers for weeks you already have data for.
  4. Log every Friday — enter each client's weekly numbers: comments, posts, outreach, applications, and follow-ups.
  5. Send the weekly report — go to a client's Report page, select the week range, and click Print / Save as PDF to send to your client.
⚠️ You can only edit your own clients. If you enter something wrong, delete the entry and re-log. The Team View button shows everyone's compliance — it's visible to all coaches.
